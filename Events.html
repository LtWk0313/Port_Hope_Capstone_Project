Main Stage Jazz Live SAT SEP 16, 2023 • 12 PM
More InfoThe Port Hope Jazz experience will have you swooning and grooving.

Originally founded in 2001, Port Hope Jazz has been a showcase for Canadian talent for over 15 years,
offering live Canadian music to the Port Hope Community as well as visitors from across Ontario.

On the fourth weekend in August, this year’s program will feature top-calibre Canadian Jazz musicians in local restaurants and businesses all around town.

Port Hope Jazz, formally the All-Canadian Jazz Festival Port Hope, is a non-profit organization dedicated to exploring and promoting Canadian jazz excellence.
